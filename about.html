Welcome to MPC

Magic Plum Company is the central organisation behind a growing family of brands and creative ventures. Established with the goal of combining innovation, media and technology, MPC serves as both a parent identity and a launchpad for future projects.

Who We Are

Magic Plum Company (MPC) is a modern holding brand focused on innovation, digital media, technology and bold ideas. We create structure for ambitious concepts, giving them a professional identity while allowing each venture to grow independently. MPC blends creativity with strategy to help brands thrive in a fast-moving online world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build memorable brands, support inclusive creativity, and create modern services that connect communities online and beyond. We believe that strong identities, thoughtful design and reliable technology can turn hobbies into respected organisations.

Our Values

Innovation, inclusion, professionalism, creativity and long-term growth are at the heart of everything we do. MPC encourages fresh ideas while maintaining quality standards across all projects.

Our Network

MPC proudly supports Femboy Media Corporation, Prism Media Group, MagicByte ICT and United Broadcasting Company. Each organisation has its own focus, audience and future roadmap, while benefiting from shared support, branding direction and strategic oversight.

What We Do

Across our network we work on media development, digital branding, web presence, ICT solutions, broadcasting concepts, design systems and community-first online experiences.

Looking Ahead

As MPC grows, we aim to expand into broadcasting, web services, content production, design and future technology projects. Our long-term vision is to become a recognised umbrella brand known for launching modern, memorable and meaningful ventures.

Partnerships & Opportunities

We welcome conversations with creators, businesses, developers and collaborators who share our values. Whether it is media, technology or design, MPC is always open to exploring the next great idea.
